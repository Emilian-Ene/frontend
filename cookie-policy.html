2. What technologies do we use and what are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device to help us remember your preferences, provide features, analyze site usage, and personalize content. We may also use your IP address and analytics scripts to understand how users interact with our site.

3. Cookie Consent Management

In compliance with GDPR and UK GDPR, we implement a consent management system that allows you to:

  • Give specific consent for different types of cookies
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Access clear information about each cookie type
  • Make granular choices about your preferences

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Your preferences are stored and respected throughout your browsing session.

4. How to manage cookies in your browser

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Browser-specific instructions:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

For more detailed instructions, visit allaboutcookies.org or your browser's help section.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR and UK GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data processed through cookies:

6.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to request information about the personal data we process about you, including data collected through cookies.

6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can request correction of inaccurate personal data and completion of incomplete data.

6.3 Right to Erasure "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your personal data when it's no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected, or when you withdraw consent.

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request restriction of processing in certain circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of data.

6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can request your personal data in a structured, commonly used format and transfer it to another service provider.

6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests, including profiling and direct marketing.

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. This includes consent for analytics and marketing cookies.

6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority:

  • EU residents: Contact your national data protection authority
  • UK residents: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) - ico.org.uk

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@topc-trading.com

Subject line: "GDPR Rights Request"

Response time: We will respond within 30 days (may be extended to 60 days for complex requests)

7. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers may be located outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom. When we transfer personal data to these countries, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Adequacy decisions: Transfers to countries deemed adequate by the European Commission

For specific information about data transfers related to our services, please contact us at privacy@topc-trading.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data collected through cookies only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

9. Automated Decision Making and Profiling

We do not use cookies for automated decision-making that produces legal effects or significantly affects you. Any profiling activities are limited to:

  • Website optimization and user experience improvement
  • Anonymous analytics for service enhancement
  • Marketing personalization (only with explicit consent)

You have the right to object to profiling activities and request human intervention in automated processes.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make material changes:

  • We will display a prominent notice on our website
